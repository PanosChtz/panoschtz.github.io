About

Hello, I’m Panagiotis (Panos). I joined Visa Research as a Research Scientist in June 2022. I completed my PhD at George Mason University in June 2022 under the supervision of Dr. Foteini Baldimtsi.

My research lies in the field of cryptography and blockchains. I initially focused on blockchain auditability, accountable payment systems, blockchain support for resource-constrained devices, and improving the usability of blockchain applications.

My more recent work focuses on account and fund recovery mechanisms in blockchains, privacy-preserving financial systems, and secure architectures for decentralized applications. I also worked as a summer intern and part-time researcher with Facebook/Novi.
